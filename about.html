Our Story

It all began when Matthew, one of our cofounders here at Damai Global, returned home from his two-year church service mission to Ghana. He came home with many new friendships that he would always talk about and how he wanted to make a lasting difference in his friends lives. One day, Matthew's Father, Dr. Van Gils had the idea to make Emnmanuel, one of Matthew's friends from Ghana a virtual assistant for his Doctor's office.

So they tried it. It was a hit. Emmanuel was a very dedicated and intelligent individual who did great work. Quickly, the managers at the Office noticed and wanted more employees like Emmanuel. Today, Damai Global is constantly striving to find more work for amazing individuals like Emmanuel who live in countries with less opoortunity. You can be apart of making a difference in the world today.
